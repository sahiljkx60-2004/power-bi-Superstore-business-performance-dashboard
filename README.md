# 📊 Superstore Business Performance Analysis | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes Superstore sales data using Power BI to uncover insights into sales performance, customer behavior, product profitability, and regional trends.

The objective is to help business stakeholders make data-driven decisions through interactive dashboards and actionable recommendations.

## 🎯 Business Problem

The management team wanted to understand:

- Which regions generate the highest revenue and profit?
- Which customer segments contribute the most value?
- Which products drive profitability?
- How do discounts affect profit?
- What strategic actions can improve business performance?

- ## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Power Query
- DAX
- Microsoft Excel

- ## 📊 Dashboard Pages

| Page | Description |
|------|-------------|
| Executive Dashboard | Sales, Profit & KPI Overview |
| Customer Analytics | Customer Segmentation & Value Analysis |
| Product Analytics | Product Performance & Profitability |
| Regional Dashboard | Regional Sales & Profit Analysis |
| Business Insights | Recommendations & Strategic Findings |

## 📈 Key Insights

- Generated total sales of 946.81K with a profit margin of 9.05%.
- Identified that 24.22% of orders were loss-making.
- Consumer segment contributed the highest sales and profit.
- Office Supplies generated the highest profit.
- West region consistently outperformed other regions.
- Discounts above 20% significantly reduced profitability.

- ## 💡 Strategic Recommendations

- Optimize discount policies to improve profit margins.
- Focus on high-performing product categories.
- Improve profitability within the Furniture category.
- Strengthen customer retention initiatives.
- Replicate successful regional strategies across lower-performing regions.
